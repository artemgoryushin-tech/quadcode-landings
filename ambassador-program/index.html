Who is this program for?

The ambassador program is for partners, consultants, affiliates, agencies, and networkers who can introduce qualified clients interested in launching their own brokerage business.

You can earn up to $13,500 for each qualified referred client.

Do I need brokerage experience to join?

No. You mainly need access to the right audience and the ability to introduce relevant prospects.
